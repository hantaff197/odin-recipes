Kimchi Fried Rice

How to make kimchi fried rice

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Kimchi
  • vegetable oil
  • sesame oil
  • gochujang
  • condiments (fried egg, seaweed)

Steps:

  1. Heat up the pan and add vegetable oil
  2. Fry the kimchi
  3. Add the rice into the pan and mix it well
  4. Add kimchi juice, gochujang, and sesame oil and mix it well
  5. Served well. You can add seaweed or fried egg for more flavor.
