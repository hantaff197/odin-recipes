Chicken Karaage

Ingredients:

  • boneless chicken tight
  • soy sauce
  • mirin
  • sake
  • potato strach
  • vegetable oil

Steps:

  1. On the bowl, add boneless chicken tight and sake to reduce the smell
  2. add soy sauce and mirin and mix it well, after that you can marinade for 15 minutes
  3. move the chicken to new plate and batter it with potato stratch
  4. on some hot oil add the battered chicken. Cook well
  5. the chicken can be served. Add some mayonaise to cut the greasiness.
